Each student at CODDYCA - Coding School for Kids & Teens, regardless of the group or class, has the right to receive points for certain achievements in academic performance. Basically, a Point is a virtual unit of measurement of student success.
1 Point = CAD $1
There are several types of points:
Leader Point:Awarded to one student only at the end of each coding class for active discussion of the material, helping other students and clean code without errors.
Kahoot Point:Awarder for the first place in the Kahoot game, in which we consolidate the studied material.
Big Brain Battle Point:After creating the game, we do a game competition, the goal of which is to win the game in the shortest possible time, collecting the maximum number of trophies. The winner of the Big Brain Battle get one point.
M.Y.O.G. Point:At the end of each month, we reserve two coding lessons to create our students' own games. After the end of game development, students show each other their work and vote for the best. How many votes a student receives with their game - so many $$$ points are converted.
Points Exchange
Points are being exchanged to the Canadian Dollars in the form of sending electronic Amazon Gift Cards or Roblox Gift Cards to the email of a Parent or Guardian specified during student registration for the regular coding classes.
